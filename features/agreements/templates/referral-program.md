# Referral Program Terms

**Effective Date:** January 10, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Program Overview

The TSmart Warehouse Referral Program ("Program") rewards users who refer new customers, warehouse owners, or partners to the Platform. By participating, you agree to these Referral Program Terms.

## 2. Eligibility

2.1. **Who Can Participate**:
- Active Platform users in good standing
- Must have completed account verification
- Must comply with Platform Terms of Service
- Not available to Platform employees or their immediate family

2.2. **Geographic Restrictions**:
- Available in Turkey and supported countries
- Void where prohibited by law
- Subject to local tax and regulatory requirements

2.3. **Account Standing**:
- Account must be active and in good standing
- No recent violations of Terms of Service
- No outstanding payment issues
- Platform reserves right to disqualify participants

## 3. How the Program Works

3.1. **Referral Process**:
1. **Get Your Link**: Unique referral link in account dashboard
2. **Share**: Share link with friends, colleagues, business contacts
3. **They Sign Up**: New user creates account using your link
4. **They Complete Action**: New user completes qualifying action
5. **You Earn Reward**: Referral credit or commission credited to your account

3.2. **Tracking**: Referrals tracked via:
- Unique referral codes/links
- Cookies (30-day attribution window)
- Email invitations (direct attribution)
- Referral code entered during signup

3.3. **Attribution Period**: 
- **Cookie Expiration**: 30 days from click
- **Email Invitation**: 90 days from send
- **Manual Code**: Must be entered at signup

## 4. Referral Types and Rewards

### 4.1 Customer Referrals

**Qualifying Action**: New customer completes first paid booking

**Referrer Reward**:
- 500 TRY Platform credit
- OR 10% of first booking value (whichever is greater)
- Maximum: 2,000 TRY per referral

**Referee Benefit**:
- 250 TRY credit toward first booking
- Applied automatically at checkout

**Conditions**:
- Must be new customer (no prior bookings)
- Booking minimum: 1,000 TRY
- Credit valid for 12 months

### 4.2 Warehouse Owner Referrals

**Qualifying Action**: New warehouse owner lists property and receives first booking

**Referrer Reward**:
- 1,000 TRY cash or Platform credit
- OR 5% of warehouse owner's first 12 months revenue
- Maximum: 10,000 TRY per referral

**Referee Benefit**:
- Waived listing fee (first 3 months)
- Priority customer support
- Featured listing for first month

**Conditions**:
- Warehouse must be approved by Platform
- Must receive at least one paid booking within 90 days
- Minimum booking value: 2,000 TRY

### 4.3 Partner Referrals (Resellers, Warehouse Finders)

**Qualifying Action**: Referred partner completes onboarding and achieves first milestone

**Referrer Reward**:
- 2,000 TRY cash bonus
- OR 2% of partner's first 6 months commissions
- Maximum: 5,000 TRY per referral

**Referee Benefit**:
- Enhanced training and onboarding
- Reduced commission split for first 3 months
- Dedicated account manager

**Conditions**:
- Partner must be approved and pass background check
- Must complete at least one successful transaction
- Must remain active for minimum 6 months

### 4.4 Volume Bonuses

**5+ Referrals** (per quarter):
- 10% bonus on all referral rewards that quarter
- Exclusive "Super Referrer" badge
- Early access to new features

**10+ Referrals** (per quarter):
- 20% bonus on all referral rewards that quarter
- Dedicated referral specialist
- Custom referral materials

**20+ Referrals** (per quarter):
- 30% bonus on all referral rewards that quarter
- Invitation to exclusive referrer events
- Potential partnership opportunities

## 5. Reward Payout

5.1. **Processing Time**:
- Rewards processed within 30 days of qualifying action
- Notification sent via email and in-app
- Available in "Rewards" section of account dashboard

5.2. **Payout Methods**:
- **Platform Credits**: Applied automatically to account
- **Cash Payout**: Bank transfer (for amounts over 1,000 TRY)
- **Choice**: Choose payout method in settings

5.3. **Minimum Payout**: 
- Platform credits: No minimum
- Cash payout: 500 TRY minimum
- Amounts below minimum roll over to next payout

5.4. **Tax Implications**:
- Rewards may be taxable income
- Referrer responsible for reporting and paying taxes
- Platform issues tax documents as required by law (Turkey: "Gelir Vergisi")
- Cash payouts may require tax withholding per Turkish law

## 6. Program Rules

6.1. **Permitted Marketing**:
- Share referral link on social media (personal accounts)
- Email referral invitations to personal contacts
- Mention Program in blog posts or content (with disclosure)
- Word-of-mouth recommendations

6.2. **Prohibited Activities**:
- **Spam**: Mass unsolicited emails, spammy social posts
- **Misleading Claims**: False or deceptive representations
- **Self-Referrals**: Creating accounts to refer yourself
- **Incentivized Signups**: Paying others to sign up using your link
- **Gaming the System**: Creating fake accounts, manipulating bookings
- **Brand Misrepresentation**: Impersonating Platform or official partner
- **Paid Advertising** (without approval): Google Ads, Facebook Ads using Platform trademarks
- **Cookie Stuffing**: Automated referral link clicking
- **URL Shorteners**: Hiding referral links in misleading URLs
- **Keyword Bidding**: Bidding on Platform's trademarked keywords in PPC ads

6.3. **Approval Required For**:
- Paid advertising campaigns featuring Platform
- Large-scale email campaigns (over 1,000 recipients)
- Affiliate marketing websites dedicated to Platform
- Content that includes Platform trademarks prominently
- Contact: partnerships@tsmart.com for approval

6.4. **Disclosure Requirements**:
- Disclose referral relationship when promoting
- Example: "This is my referral link. I may earn a reward if you sign up."
- Required by consumer protection laws

## 7. Referral Tracking and Reporting

7.1. **Dashboard Access**:
- View all referrals in real-time
- Track referral status (pending, qualified, credited)
- See earnings and available rewards
- Download referral reports

7.2. **Referral Statuses**:
- **Pending**: User signed up but hasn't completed qualifying action
- **Qualified**: Qualifying action completed, reward processing
- **Credited**: Reward paid out
- **Expired**: Attribution period expired without qualifying action
- **Disqualified**: Referral didn't meet requirements or violated rules

7.3. **Disputes**: If you believe referral wasn't credited:
- Contact support@tsmart.com within 30 days
- Provide: Referral link, referee email, signup date, qualifying action date
- Platform investigates and responds within 15 business days

## 8. Limitations and Restrictions

8.1. **Caps**:
- Maximum 100 active referrals per quarter
- Maximum 50,000 TRY in referral earnings per year
- Enterprise referral programs available for high-volume referrers

8.2. **Exclusions**:
- Referrals who were previous Platform users (inactive over 12 months may qualify)
- Bookings made using promotional credits only (must have cash payment component)
- Test or demo bookings
- Refunded or cancelled bookings (reward clawed back)
- Fraudulent accounts or transactions

8.3. **Geographic**: 
- Referee must be in supported country
- Some regions may have different reward amounts

8.4. **Timing**:
- Qualifying action must occur within attribution period
- Reward earned when action completed, not when referred

## 9. Changes and Termination

9.1. **Program Changes**: Platform may:
- Change reward amounts
- Modify qualifying actions
- Add or remove referral types
- Update Program rules

9.2. **Notice**: Material changes communicated 30 days in advance.

9.3. **Grandfathering**: Pending referrals honored under terms when referral link was created.

9.4. **Program Termination**: Platform may terminate Program:
- With 90 days notice
- Outstanding rewards honored
- New referrals not accepted after termination date

9.5. **Individual Termination**: Platform may terminate your participation:
- For rule violations
- For fraud or abuse
- For Terms of Service violations
- Immediate effect with forfeiture of pending rewards

## 10. Fraud Prevention

10.1. **Monitoring**: Platform monitors for:
- Unusual referral patterns
- Self-referrals
- Fake accounts
- Coordinated fraud

10.2. **Consequences of Fraud**:
- Immediate disqualification from Program
- Forfeiture of all pending and paid rewards
- Account suspension or termination
- Legal action for significant fraud
- Reporting to authorities if appropriate

10.3. **Good Faith**: Participate honestly and ethically. If in doubt, ask: referrals@tsmart.com

## 11. Clawbacks and Refunds

11.1. **Clawback Triggers**:
- Referred user cancels within 30 days and receives full refund
- Fraud or Terms of Service violation discovered
- Chargeback on referred booking
- Referred account closed before completing one successful transaction

11.2. **Clawback Process**:
- Referral reward reversed
- Platform credit deducted from account
- Cash payout may require repayment
- Notification provided with explanation

11.3. **Negative Balances**:
- If clawbacks exceed available credits, balance may go negative
- Must be resolved before future payouts
- May be deducted from future referral earnings
