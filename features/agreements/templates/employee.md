## 7. Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

7.1. **Confidential Information**: Employee agrees to protect:
- **Business Information**: Customer lists, pricing, strategies, financials
- **Technical Information**: Software code, algorithms, designs
- **Product Information**: Roadmaps, unreleased features
- **HR Information**: Salaries, employee data

- Not use confidential information for personal benefit
- Return all confidential materials upon termination

9.1. **Non-Compete** (to extent permitted by law):
- During employment and for [6-12] months after termination
- Employee will not work for direct competitors
- Scope: [Specific competing companies or activities]
- Geography: [Reasonable geographic area]

9.2. **Non-Solicitation**:

12.1. **Grounds for Discipline**:
- Performance issues
- Policy violations
- Misconduct or inappropriate behavior
- Breach of this Agreement

12.2. **Disciplinary Process**:
- **Verbal Warning**: For minor first offenses
- **Written Warning**: For repeated or more serious issues
- **Suspension**: For significant violations (with or without pay)
- **Termination**: For severe violations or repeated issues

12.3. **Immediate Termination**: Company may immediately terminate for:
- Serious misconduct (theft, fraud, violence, harassment)
- Gross negligence
- Breach of confidentiality causing significant harm
- Criminal activity
- Refusing to perform duties

12.4. **Termination by Company**:
- **With Cause**: Immediate, with cause specified, severance per law only
- **Without Cause**: [Notice period per Turkish Labor Law or X days], severance per law
- **Probation**: 1 week notice (either party)
- **Force Majeure**: Economic necessity, restructuring (per Turkish Labor Law)

12.5. **Termination by Employee**:

12.6. **Severance**: Per Turkish Labor Law requirements based on tenure.

## 13. Post-Termination Obligations

13.1. **Return of Property**: Upon termination, Employee must return:
- Company laptop, phone, equipment
- Access badges, keys
- Documents, files (physical and digital)
- Any Company property in Employee's possession

13.2. **Final Pay**:
- Paid within legal timeline
- Includes: Unpaid salary, accrued vacation, severance (if applicable)
- Deductions: Outstanding debts to Company (if any)

13.3. **Continuing Obligations**:
- Confidentiality obligations continue (5 years)
- Non-compete and non-solicitation (per Section 9)
- Intellectual property assignments remain valid
- Cooperation with legal matters or investigations

13.4. **References**: Company will provide neutral reference confirming dates of employment and title.

14.1. **Personal Data Collection**: Company collects and processes Employee's personal data:

14.3. **Data Rights** (KVKK): Employee has rights to:

14.4. **Data Security**: Company implements appropriate security measures.

14.5. **Data Retention**: Employee data retained per legal requirements (typically 10 years after termination).
