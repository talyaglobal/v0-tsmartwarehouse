# Insurance & Liability Policy

**Effective Date:** January 10, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Policy Overview

This Insurance & Liability Policy ("Policy") outlines insurance requirements, liability limitations, and risk allocation between TSmart Warehouse ("Platform"), Warehouse Owners ("Providers"), and Customers for warehouse storage services.

## 2. Platform's Role and Liability

2.1. **Marketplace Function**: Platform operates as a marketplace connecting Customers with Warehouse Owners. Platform does not own, operate, or directly provide warehouse storage services.

2.2. **Limited Liability**: Platform's liability is limited to:
- Technical failures of the Platform itself
- Unauthorized access to user accounts due to Platform's security breach
- Processing errors in payments handled directly by Platform

2.3. **Liability Exclusions**: Platform is NOT liable for:
- Loss, damage, or theft of stored goods
- Quality or condition of warehouse facilities
- Acts or omissions of Warehouse Owners or their staff
- Delays in delivery or access to stored goods
- Business interruption or consequential damages
- Claims between Customers and Warehouse Owners

2.4. **Maximum Platform Liability**: Platform's maximum liability for any claim is limited to:
- The lesser of: (a) total fees paid by claimant in past 12 months, or (b) 10,000 TRY

## 3. Warehouse Owner Insurance Requirements

3.1. **Mandatory Coverage**: All Warehouse Owners must maintain:

**Property Insurance:**
- Coverage: Building and facility damage
- Minimum: Replacement value of facility
- Perils: Fire, flood, earthquake, vandalism, etc.

**General Liability Insurance:**
- Coverage: Third-party bodily injury and property damage
- Minimum: 2,000,000 TRY per occurrence
- Aggregate: 5,000,000 TRY annual aggregate

**Warehouse Legal Liability / Bailee Insurance:**
- Coverage: Customer goods in care, custody, and control
- Minimum: 5,000,000 TRY per occurrence
- Coverage includes fire, theft, water damage, and other specified perils

**Workers' Compensation Insurance** (if applicable):
- Coverage: Employee injuries
- Required by Turkish law for all employees

3.2. **Proof of Insurance**: Warehouse Owners must:
- Upload current insurance certificates to Platform
- Update certificates before expiration
- Name Platform as additional insured on liability policies
- Provide 30 days notice of cancellation or material changes

3.3. **Verification**: Platform verifies insurance coverage:
- Upon initial registration
- Annually upon renewal
- As requested by Customers
- After any reported incidents

3.4. **Suspension for Non-Compliance**: Warehouse listings may be suspended if:
- Insurance lapses or expires
- Coverage falls below minimum requirements
- Proof of insurance not provided within 30 days of request

## 4. Customer Insurance Options

4.1. **Platform Insurance Program**: Customers may purchase additional insurance through Platform's partner program:

**Basic Coverage:**
- Premium: 1.5% of declared value per month
- Maximum: 100,000 TRY per booking
- Covers: Fire, theft, water damage, collapse
- Deductible: 500 TRY

**Enhanced Coverage:**
- Premium: 2.5% of declared value per month
- Maximum: 500,000 TRY per booking
- Covers: All-risk coverage including negligence
- Deductible: 1,000 TRY
- Additional benefits: Business interruption, transit coverage

**Premium Coverage:**
- Premium: 4% of declared value per month
- Maximum: 2,000,000 TRY per booking
- Covers: Comprehensive all-risk
- Deductible: None
- Additional benefits: Full business interruption, worldwide transit, refrigeration breakdown

4.2. **Own Insurance**: Customers may:
- Use their own commercial insurance
- Must provide certificate naming Warehouse Owner and Platform
- Responsibility to ensure adequate coverage

4.3. **Self-Insurance**: Customers may decline insurance coverage:
- Must acknowledge and accept risk
- Signed waiver required
- Cannot later claim inadequate coverage notice

## 5. Declared Value and Coverage Limits

5.1. **Declared Value**: Customers must declare:
- Total value of goods to be stored
- Description of goods (general categories acceptable)
- Any high-value items (over 10,000 TRY each)

5.2. **Under-Declaration**: If declared value is less than actual value:
- Claims paid on pro-rata basis
- Example: If declared 50% of actual value, claim paid at 50%

5.3. **Over-Declaration**: Premium refund if proven value is significantly less than declared.

5.4. **High-Value Items**: Items over 10,000 TRY each require:
- Individual declaration
- Description and serial numbers (if applicable)
- Possible additional premium or approval

5.5. **Excluded Items**: The following are typically NOT covered or require special underwriting:
- Cash, negotiable instruments, precious metals (except as specifically declared)
- Hazardous materials
- Perishable goods (unless in climate-controlled facility with appropriate coverage)
- Artwork and antiques over 50,000 TRY (require appraisal)
- Illegal or prohibited items

## 6. Claims Process

6.1. **Notification**: In case of loss or damage:
- **Immediate**: Notify Warehouse Owner and Platform within 24 hours of discovery
- **Documentation**: Preserve evidence, take photos, document damage
- **Police Report**: Required for theft or vandalism
- **Formal Claim**: Submit written claim within 7 days

6.2. **Claim Documentation**: Submit:
- Completed claim form
- Photos/videos of damage
- Proof of ownership (receipts, invoices, etc.)
- Police report (if applicable)
- Estimate of repair cost or replacement value
- Any other requested documentation

6.3. **Investigation**: 
- Insurer investigates claim within 10 business days
- May inspect damaged goods
- May interview involved parties
- May request additional documentation

6.4. **Resolution Timeline**:
- **Simple Claims**: Resolved within 30 days
- **Complex Claims**: Up to 90 days
- **Disputed Claims**: May require mediation or litigation

6.5. **Payment**: Approved claims paid within 15 business days of approval.

## 7. Liability Allocation

7.1. **Warehouse Owner Liability**: Warehouse Owner is liable for:
- Negligence in security, maintenance, or operations
- Breach of storage contract terms
- Employee misconduct or criminal acts
- Failure to maintain insurance
- Misrepresentation of facility conditions

7.2. **Customer Liability**: Customer is liable for:
- Accurate description and declaration of goods
- Proper packaging and preparation of goods
- Compliance with storage terms and regulations
- Payment of storage fees
- Damage caused by Customer's goods (e.g., hazardous materials, infestation)

7.3. **Shared Liability**: In some cases, liability may be shared:
- Apportioned based on fault percentages
- Determined through negotiation, mediation, or arbitration

7.4. **Force Majeure**: No party liable for losses due to:
- Acts of God (earthquake, flood, etc.)
- War, terrorism, civil unrest
- Government actions beyond control
- Other unforeseeable events beyond reasonable control
- (Unless specific insurance coverage applies)

## 8. Indemnification

8.1. **Customer Indemnification**: Customer indemnifies Platform and Warehouse Owner against:
- Claims arising from Customer's goods
- Customer's breach of agreement
- Illegal or prohibited items stored
- Third-party claims related to Customer's goods

8.2. **Warehouse Owner Indemnification**: Warehouse Owner indemnifies Platform and Customer against:
- Warehouse Owner's negligence
- Facility defects or unsafe conditions
- Employee misconduct
- Breach of insurance requirements

8.3. **Platform Indemnification**: Platform indemnifies users against:
- Platform's gross negligence or willful misconduct
- Breach of data protection obligations
- Unauthorized access due to Platform's security failures

## 9. Risk Mitigation and Safety

9.1. **Warehouse Owner Obligations**:
- Maintain safe and secure facility
- Implement fire prevention and detection systems
- Control access to stored goods
- Regular facility inspections and maintenance
- Proper staff training
- Pest control measures
- Climate control (if represented)

9.2. **Customer Obligations**:
- Properly package and label goods
- Disclose any special requirements or hazards
- Comply with safety regulations
- Remove goods promptly upon contract end
- Maintain valid insurance

9.3. **Inspections**: Platform may:
- Conduct facility safety audits
- Require corrective actions
- Suspend listings for safety violations

## 10. Subrogation

10.1. **Insurer's Rights**: Upon paying a claim, insurer may pursue subrogation against responsible parties.

10.2. **Cooperation**: Insured party must cooperate with subrogation efforts:
- Provide information and documentation
- Participate in legal proceedings if required
- Not prejudice insurer's subrogation rights

10.3. **Customer Assignment**: By filing a claim, Customer assigns rights to insurer to recover from negligent parties.

## 11. Specific Perils and Coverages

11.1. **Fire and Explosion**: 
- Covered under standard policies
- Customer must not store flammable materials without disclosure and approval

11.2. **Water Damage**:
- Covered if due to plumbing failure, roof leak, or flooding
- May be excluded if facility is in known flood zone without flood insurance

11.3. **Theft and Burglary**:
- Covered if facility has adequate security
- Customer must provide evidence of value
- Mysterious disappearance typically NOT covered

11.4. **Negligence**:
- Warehouse Owner's negligence covered under liability policies
- Customer's own negligence (improper packaging) may not be covered

11.5. **Natural Disasters**:
- Earthquake: May require separate coverage
- Flood: Typically separate policy required
- Wind/Storm: Generally covered

11.6. **Collapse**:
- Building collapse covered if not due to Customer's goods or excluded causes

11.7. **Vandalism and Malicious Mischief**: Covered under standard policies.

11.8. **Vermin, Insects, Mold**:
- Typically NOT covered unless due to Warehouse Owner's negligence in maintenance
- Customer responsible for proper packaging to prevent infestation

## 12. Business Interruption Coverage

12.1. **Availability**: Optional add-on coverage for businesses relying on stored inventory.

12.2. **Coverage**: Reimburses lost business income if goods are damaged and business cannot operate.

12.3. **Requirements**:
- Proof of business operations
- Financial records showing typical revenue
- Direct link between stored goods and business operations

12.4. **Waiting Period**: Typically 72 hours after loss before coverage begins.

## 13. Transit and Transportation Insurance

13.1. **Transit Coverage**: If Customer uses Platform's transportation service:
- Goods covered door-to-door
- Insurance included in transportation fee
- Maximum: 100,000 TRY unless additional coverage purchased

13.2. **Customer Arranged Transit**: If Customer arranges own transport:
- Customer responsible for transit insurance
- Warehouse Owner's coverage begins when goods arrive at facility

13.3. **Loading/Unloading**: Coverage during loading/unloading typically included in warehouse policy.

## 14. Liability Limitations and Disclaimers

14.1. **Consequential Damages**: No party is liable for:
- Lost profits
- Business interruption (unless specifically insured)
- Indirect or consequential damages
- Punitive damages (except for gross negligence or willful misconduct)

14.2. **Maximum Liability**: Unless higher limits purchased:
- Warehouse Owner's liability limited to lesser of: (a) declared value, or (b) 10x monthly storage fee
- Platform's liability limited per Section 2.4

14.3. **Time Limits**: Claims must be filed within:
- Property damage: 2 years from date of loss
- Personal injury: 3 years from date of incident
- Contract disputes: 5 years from breach

14.4. **Disclaimer of Warranties**: Warehouse Owner provides storage "AS-IS" without warranties except as specifically stated in storage agreement.

## 15. Insurance for International Customers

15.1. **Cross-Border Coverage**: International customers should ensure:
- Insurance valid in Turkey
- Currency coverage adequate (exchange rate considerations)
- Insurer can pay claims in Turkey

15.2. **Additional Considerations**:
- Customs and import/export regulations
- Transit insurance for international shipments
- Political risk coverage if applicable

## 16. High-Value and Special Goods

16.1. **Definition**: Goods over 100,000 TRY total value or 10,000 TRY per item.

16.2. **Special Requirements**:
- Pre-approval required
- Detailed inventory with serial numbers
- Photos or appraisals
- Enhanced security measures may be required
- Higher insurance premium

16.3. **Special Coverage Options**:
- Agreed value (vs. actual cash value)
- Scheduled item coverage
- Blanket coverage for multiple items

## 20. Definitions

**Declared Value**: The total value of goods as stated by Customer.

**Actual Cash Value**: Replacement cost minus depreciation.

**Replacement Cost**: Cost to replace with new item of similar kind and quality.

**Deductible**: Amount Customer pays before insurance coverage applies.

**Per Occurrence**: Maximum payout for each single incident.

**Aggregate**: Maximum total payout over policy period (typically 12 months).

**Named Insured**: Party specifically listed on insurance policy.

**Additional Insured**: Party added to policy for protection (e.g., Platform on Warehouse Owner's policy).

**Subrogation**: Insurer's right to pursue recovery from responsible third parties after paying a claim.
