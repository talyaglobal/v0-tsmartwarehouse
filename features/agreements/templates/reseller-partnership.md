# Reseller Partnership Agreement

**Effective Date:** January 10, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Agreement Overview

This Reseller Partnership Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between TSmart Warehouse ("Company") and the Reseller ("Partner"). This Agreement governs the terms under which Partner may resell Company's warehouse storage solutions and services.

## 2. Appointment as Reseller

2.1. **Authorization**: Company hereby appoints Partner as a non-exclusive reseller of Company's services within the agreed territory.

2.2. **Territory**: Partner's sales territory is defined as: [To be specified upon agreement acceptance]

2.3. **Term**: This Agreement is effective from the date of acceptance and continues for an initial term of 12 months, automatically renewable unless terminated by either party.

## 3. Partner Responsibilities

3.1. **Sales Activities**: Partner agrees to:
- Actively promote and market Company's services
- Maintain professional standards in all customer interactions
- Accurately represent Company's services and pricing
- Submit leads and opportunities through the CRM system
- Provide regular sales reports and forecasts

3.2. **Compliance**: Partner must:
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Maintain required business licenses and permits
- Follow Company's brand guidelines and marketing standards
- Protect confidential information and trade secrets
- Not make unauthorized commitments on Company's behalf

3.3. **Customer Support**: Partner will provide first-level customer support and escalate complex issues to Company.

## 4. Commission Structure

4.1. **Commission Rates**:
- New Customer (First Year): 15% of total contract value
- Renewal Business: 10% of renewal contract value
- Upsell/Cross-sell: 12% of additional revenue

4.2. **Payment Terms**:
- Commissions calculated monthly
- Payment within 30 days of invoice approval
- Minimum payout threshold: $100
- Payment via bank transfer or agreed method

4.3. **Commission Adjustments**:
- Full refunds: 100% commission clawback
- Partial refunds: Proportional commission adjustment
- Chargebacks: Commission reversal

## 5. Company Responsibilities

5.1. **Support**: Company will provide:
- Sales training and onboarding materials
- Marketing collateral and product documentation
- CRM access and lead management tools
- Technical support for Partner's questions
- Regular product updates and roadmap information

5.2. **Lead Distribution**: Company may distribute inbound leads to Partners based on territory and performance metrics.

5.3. **Deal Registration**: Registered deals are protected for 90 days, giving Partner exclusive rights to work the opportunity.

## 6. Pricing and Quotations

6.1. **Pricing Authority**: All final pricing must be approved by Company. Partner may not offer unauthorized discounts exceeding [X]% without prior approval.

6.2. **Quotation Process**: Partner must use Company's official quotation system for all customer proposals.

6.3. **Price Protection**: Company will honor quoted prices for 30 days from quotation date.

## 7. Marketing and Branding

7.1. **Brand Usage**: Partner may use Company's trademarks and logos only as specified in brand guidelines.

7.2. **Marketing Materials**: Company provides approved marketing materials. Partner must seek approval for custom materials.

7.3. **Co-Marketing**: Opportunities for co-marketing initiatives subject to mutual agreement and budget availability.

## 8. Performance Metrics

8.1. **Minimum Performance**: Partner is expected to generate minimum quarterly revenue of [Amount] to maintain active status.

8.2. **Performance Reviews**: Quarterly business reviews to assess performance and plan future activities.

8.3. **Tier Structure**: 
- Bronze: $0-50K quarterly revenue (12% base commission)
- Silver: $50K-150K quarterly revenue (15% base commission)
- Gold: $150K+ quarterly revenue (18% base commission + bonus incentives)

## 9. Confidentiality

9.1. **Confidential Information**: Partner agrees to maintain confidentiality of:
- Customer data and contact information
- Pricing structures and discount schedules
- Product roadmaps and strategic plans
- Technical specifications and documentation
- Financial information and business metrics

9.2. **Non-Disclosure**: Partner will not disclose confidential information to third parties without prior written consent.

9.3. **Return of Information**: Upon termination, Partner must return or destroy all confidential materials.

## 10. Non-Compete and Non-Solicitation

10.1. **Non-Compete**: During the term and for 12 months after termination, Partner will not directly compete with Company in the warehouse storage solutions market within their territory.

10.2. **Non-Solicitation**: Partner will not solicit Company's employees, contractors, or other partners for 12 months after termination.

10.3. **Customer Protection**: Customers acquired through this partnership remain Company's customers. Partner may not redirect customers to competing services.

## 11. Termination

11.1. **Termination for Cause**: Either party may terminate immediately for:
- Material breach of this Agreement
- Bankruptcy or insolvency
- Fraud or misrepresentation
- Violation of laws or regulations
- Damage to brand reputation

11.2. **Termination for Convenience**: Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice.

11.3. **Post-Termination**:
- Partner must cease using Company's trademarks immediately
- Pending commissions for completed transactions will be paid
- Pipeline deals may be reassigned at Company's discretion
- Customer relationships transfer to Company

## 12. Intellectual Property

12.1. **Ownership**: All intellectual property related to Company's services, platform, and materials remains Company's exclusive property.

12.2. **License Grant**: Company grants Partner a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use Company's trademarks solely for authorized resale activities.

12.3. **Restrictions**: Partner may not:
- Modify or create derivative works
- Reverse engineer Company's platform
- Sublicense or transfer rights to third parties

## 13. Liability and Indemnification

13.1. **Limitation of Liability**: Company's liability is limited to the total commissions paid to Partner in the 12 months preceding the claim.

13.2. **Indemnification**: Partner agrees to indemnify Company against claims arising from:
- Partner's breach of this Agreement
- Partner's negligence or misconduct
- Unauthorized representations or warranties
- Violation of third-party rights

13.3. **Insurance**: Partner is encouraged to maintain appropriate business insurance coverage.

## 14. Dispute Resolution

14.1. **Negotiation**: Parties will attempt to resolve disputes through good-faith negotiation.

14.2. **Mediation**: If negotiation fails, disputes will be submitted to mediation before pursuing legal action.

14.3. **Arbitration**: Unresolved disputes will be settled through binding arbitration in accordance with [Arbitration Rules].

14.4. **Governing Law**: This Agreement is governed by the laws of [Jurisdiction].

## 15. General Provisions

15.1. **Independent Contractor**: Partner is an independent contractor, not an employee or agent of Company.

15.2. **Entire Agreement**: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between parties and supersedes all prior agreements.

15.3. **Amendments**: Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

15.4. **Assignment**: Partner may not assign this Agreement without Company's prior written consent.

15.5. **Severability**: If any provision is invalid, the remaining provisions remain in effect.

15.6. **Waiver**: Failure to enforce any provision does not constitute a waiver of that provision.
