# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Effective Date:** January 10, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Policy Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP" or "Policy") governs the use of TSmart Warehouse ("Platform") services. By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to comply with this Policy. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.

## 2. Purpose

This Policy ensures that:
- Platform is used lawfully and ethically
- All users have a safe and secure experience
- Platform reputation and integrity are maintained
- Platform complies with legal and regulatory requirements

## 3. Permitted Uses

3.1. **Authorized Activities**: You may use the Platform to:
- Search for and book warehouse storage space
- Manage warehouse listings (if you are a warehouse owner)
- Generate leads and manage sales pipeline (if you are a reseller or warehouse finder)
- Communicate with other users through Platform tools
- Access your account data and reports
- Integrate with Platform API (per API Terms)

3.2. **Commercial Use**: Commercial use is permitted for authorized business purposes related to warehouse storage services.

3.3. **Fair Use**: You must use Platform resources reasonably and not in a manner that impairs service for other users.

## 4. Prohibited Activities

4.1. **Illegal Activities**: You may NOT use the Platform to:
- Violate any local, national, or international law or regulation
- Infringe intellectual property rights
- Engage in fraudulent activities or misrepresentation
- Facilitate money laundering or terrorist financing
- Store or transport illegal goods or contraband
- Evade taxes or customs duties
- Violate import/export regulations

4.2. **Prohibited Content**: You may NOT upload, post, or transmit:

**Illegal Content:**
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Content promoting terrorism or violence
- Content that violates any law

**Harmful Content:**
- Malware, viruses, trojans, or malicious code
- Content designed to harm or exploit users
- Phishing attempts or social engineering attacks

**Abusive Content:**
- Harassment, threats, or intimidation
- Hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Defamatory, libelous, or slanderous content
- Doxxing (publishing private information without consent)

**Adult Content:**
- Pornography or sexually explicit material
- Content exploiting or sexualizing minors in any way

**Misleading Content:**
- False, misleading, or deceptive information
- Fake reviews or testimonials
- Impersonation of others
- Misleading warehouse descriptions or photos

**Spam and Scams:**
- Unsolicited commercial messages or advertisements
- Pyramid schemes, Ponzi schemes, or other fraudulent schemes
- Phishing or credential harvesting
- Fake offers or bait-and-switch tactics

4.3. **Prohibited Goods for Storage**: You may NOT store:

**Illegal Items:**
- Illegal drugs or controlled substances
- Stolen goods
- Counterfeit goods
- Unlicensed firearms or weapons (where prohibited by law)

**Hazardous Materials** (unless specifically authorized):
- Explosives, ammunition
- Flammable liquids or gases
- Toxic or poisonous substances
- Radioactive materials
- Corrosives or oxidizers
- Biological hazards

**Restricted Items:**
- Perishable food (unless in approved refrigerated facility)
- Live animals
- Human remains
- Cash or negotiable instruments (unless in secure, approved facility)

**Dangerous Items:**
- Items that could cause harm to warehouse staff or other stored goods
- Items with offensive odors
- Pest-infested items

**Note:** If you need to store specialized items, contact support@tsmart.com for approval and specialized facility recommendations.

4.4. **Account Abuse**: You may NOT:
- Create multiple accounts to evade restrictions or manipulate systems
- Share account credentials with unauthorized users
- Use another user's account without permission
- Sell, transfer, or trade accounts
- Create accounts using false information

4.5. **System Abuse**: You may NOT:
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to Platform systems, user accounts, or data
- Use automated tools (bots, scrapers, etc.) without written permission
- Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble Platform software
- Introduce viruses, malware, or harmful code
- Attempt to circumvent security measures
- Overload or attack Platform infrastructure (DDoS, brute force, etc.)
- Interfere with other users' use of the Platform
- Exploit bugs or vulnerabilities (must report to security@tsmart.com)

4.6. **Data Misuse**: You may NOT:
- Scrape, harvest, or collect user data without authorization
- Use Platform data for purposes outside the Terms of Service
- Sell or trade user data
- Share confidential business information without permission
- Violate data protection laws (KVKK, GDPR, etc.)

4.7. **Commercial Abuse**: You may NOT:
- Use Platform for purposes other than intended warehouse storage marketplace
- Conduct competing marketplace activities using Platform data
- Poach Platform users for competing services
- Use Platform solely to generate leads for external services
- Manipulate pricing, reviews, or ratings
- Engage in bid rigging or price fixing

4.8. **Payment Abuse**: You may NOT:
- Use stolen or fraudulent payment methods
- Initiate unauthorized chargebacks
- Engage in payment fraud or money laundering
- Use Platform to process payments for unrelated transactions

## 5. User Content Responsibilities

5.1. **Ownership and Rights**: You represent that:
- You own or have rights to all content you upload
- Your content does not infringe third-party rights
- You have authority to enter into agreements on behalf of your company (if applicable)

5.2. **Content Accuracy**: You must:
- Provide accurate warehouse descriptions and specifications
- Upload genuine, unaltered photos
- Disclose material defects or limitations
- Update information promptly when it changes
- Not exaggerate or misrepresent offerings

5.3. **Content Moderation**: Platform may:
- Review, moderate, or remove user content
- Request verification of claims or information
- Edit content for clarity or compliance (with notice)
- Remove content that violates this Policy

## 6. Communication Standards

6.1. **Professional Conduct**: In all Platform communications, you must:
- Be respectful and professional
- Use appropriate language
- Respond to inquiries in a timely manner
- Resolve disputes amicably
- Follow Platform's dispute resolution process

6.2. **Prohibited Communication**:
- Harassment, abuse, or threats
- Spam or unsolicited advertising
- Requests to transact outside the Platform (to evade fees)
- Sharing contact information prematurely (before booking confirmation)
- Discriminatory language or behavior

6.3. **Off-Platform Transactions**: While not strictly prohibited post-booking, users are encouraged to:
- Keep transactions on-Platform for protection
- Use Platform escrow for large transactions
- Document agreements through Platform tools
- Report suspicious off-Platform requests

## 7. Review and Rating Guidelines

7.1. **Honest Reviews**: Reviews must:
- Be based on genuine experience
- Be honest and accurate
- Relate to the actual service received
- Not contain prohibited content

7.2. **Prohibited Review Practices**:
- Fake or purchased reviews
- Reviews in exchange for compensation
- Reviews by competitors to harm business
- Retaliatory negative reviews
- Reviews containing personal information or defamation
- Blackmail or extortion using reviews

7.3. **Review Removal**: Platform may remove reviews that:
- Violate this Policy
- Are demonstrably false
- Contain prohibited content
- Are suspected to be fake or manipulated

## 8. Warehouse Owner Specific Rules

8.1. **Listing Accuracy**: Warehouse owners must:
- Provide accurate descriptions and specifications
- Upload genuine photos of the actual facility
- Disclose any limitations or issues
- Keep availability calendar updated
- Honor confirmed bookings

8.2. **Service Quality**: Warehouse owners must:
- Maintain facility in safe and secure condition
- Provide advertised amenities and services
- Allow customer access per agreement
- Handle goods with reasonable care
- Maintain required insurance coverage

8.3. **Non-Discrimination**: Warehouse owners must not:
- Discriminate based on protected characteristics
- Refuse service for unlawful reasons
- Apply different terms to different customers without legitimate reason

## 9. Customer Specific Rules

9.1. **Property Respect**: Customers must:
- Treat warehouse property with respect
- Follow warehouse rules and regulations
- Not damage property or equipment
- Clean up after deliveries and pickups
- Report any damage or issues promptly

9.2. **Declared Goods**: Customers must:
- Accurately declare goods to be stored
- Not store prohibited items
- Disclose special handling requirements
- Maintain appropriate insurance

9.3. **Payment Obligations**: Customers must:
- Pay fees on time

- Provide valid payment methods
- Not engage in payment fraud

## 10. Partner Rules (Resellers & Warehouse Finders)

10.1. **Professional Standards**: Partners must:

12.2. **Authorized Use**: You may:

## 13. Reporting Violations

13.1. **How to Report**: If you encounter Policy violations:
- **Email:** abuse@tsmart.com
- **Report Button:** Use in-app reporting tools
- **Emergency:** For immediate safety concerns, contact local authorities first, then Platform

13.2. **Information to Provide**:
- Description of violation
- User or listing involved
- Screenshots or evidence (if available)
- Date and time of violation
- Your contact information (for follow-up)

13.3. **Platform Response**:
- Acknowledgment within 24 hours
- Investigation within 5 business days
- Action taken if violation confirmed
- Confidentiality maintained (reporter identity protected)

## 14. Enforcement and Consequences

14.1. **Investigation**: Platform may:
- Investigate suspected violations
- Request information from users
- Review account activity and communications
- Consult with legal or security experts

14.2. **Actions for Violations**:

**Warning:**
- First-time minor violations
- Accidental violations
- Opportunity to correct behavior

**Temporary Suspension:**
- Repeated minor violations
- Single moderate violation
- Pending investigation of serious allegation

**Account Termination:**
- Serious violations (illegal activity, fraud, safety threats)
- Repeated violations after warnings
- Material breach of Terms or this Policy

**Legal Action:**
- Illegal activities reported to authorities
- Civil action for damages
- Cooperation with law enforcement
- Injunctive relief if necessary

14.3. **Appeal Process**:
- User may appeal within 30 days
- Submit to: appeals@tsmart.com
- Include: Account details, explanation, supporting evidence
- Review by independent team member
- Decision communicated within 15 business days
- Final decision is binding
